Our Services

Coating Solutions for Every Challenge

From tank linings to intumescent fire protection — we've applied coatings in almost every environment imaginable.

Tank Lining

Internal lining systems for water tanks, anaerobic digesters, and chemical storage. Polyurea, epoxy, and specialist chemistries.

Bund Lining

Secondary containment coatings for fuel, chemical, and process bunds. Full compliance with environmental regulations.

Industrial Flooring

High-performance resin flooring for warehouses, factories, and commercial environments. Slip-resistant, chemical-resistant, built to last.

Pipe Coating

Internal and external pipe coating systems for corrosion protection and flow improvement.

Manhole & Sewer Lining

Rehabilitation of manholes and sewer structures suffering from chemical attack and water infiltration.

Waterproofing

Membrane systems for roofs, basements, and below-grade structures. Keeping water out, permanently.

Intumescent Coatings

Passive fire protection coatings for structural steel. Certified systems, expert application.

Surface Preparation

UHP water jetting, abrasive blasting, and mechanical preparation. The foundation of every successful coating.

Cement Repairs

Substrate repair and reinstatement prior to coating application. Smooth, clean, ready for protection.
